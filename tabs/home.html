Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the bioengineering department at Stanford University. I am currently co-advised by Eric T. Kool and H. Tom Soh.

My current research primarily focuses on developing small-molecule-binding aptamers through high-throughput screening and the mechanistic study of aptamer-based biosensors.

I received a B.S. and M.S. from Tokyo Institute of Technology.
