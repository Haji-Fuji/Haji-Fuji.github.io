Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the bioengineering department at Stanford University (since fall 2022). My current advisors are H. Tom Soh and Eric T. Kool.

My current research primarily focuses on developing small-molecule-binding aptamers through high-throughput screening and the mechanistic study of aptamer-based biosensors.

I am from Japan, and my name in 漢字 is 藤田 (Fujita) 創 (Hajime).

I received a B.E. and M.E. from Tokyo Institute of Technology. My advisor during the B.E. and M.E. programs was Toshinori Fujie. Back then, I was fortunate to collaborate with Michinao Hashimoto (SUTD).
